Glass Animals

Members

  • David Algernon Bayley - Lead vocals, guitar, tambourine
  • Andrew L. MacFarlane - Guitar, keys, backing vocals
  • Edmund Irwin-Singer - Bass, keys, backing vocals
  • Joseph Seaward - Drums

Albums

  • Leaflings (2012) - This is the band's first EP consisting of 5 songs. It is a great indie, synth, pop influenced album.
  • Zabba (2014) - This album is the one that put the band on the map. It had for singles released from it that all topped the alternative rock charts.
  • How to be a Human Being (2016) - Another amazing album from these guys. It also had 4 singles that topped the charts. Interesting thing about this album is that each song relates to a character on the cover.
